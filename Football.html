    Dream. Train. Play Abroad.

    We help Indian footballers unlock global opportunities — from elite training camps to pro trials across Europe.

    Through years of scouting and partnerships, FlyInverse has built a strong international network across Spain. Our reach extends to football academies, UEFA-licensed coaches, trial programs, and semi-professional clubs who are genuinely looking for fresh talent. One of our core strengths is our exclusive partnership with Zodiac Pro Agency, a reputed Barcelona-based football agency. As their official representative in India, we open access to a reliable and verified network of football opportunities in Europe — with mentorship, guidance, and player-first support at every step.
